# Mistral — Privacy / Training Policy Evidence

**Last Verified:** 2026-04-27
**Source:** https://mistral.ai/terms/ + https://mistral.ai/privacy/

## Official Quotes

> "We do not use your prompts and completions to train or improve our models, unless you explicitly opt in to do so."
> — Mistral AI Terms of Service / Privacy Policy (retrieved 2026-04-27 via WebSearch)

## Assessment

- Mistral **does not** train on API user data by default.
- Opt-in is required for any training data contribution.
- This applies to both the free Experiment plan and paid tiers.
- La Plateforme (the API service) is GDPR-compliant (Mistral is a French company).
