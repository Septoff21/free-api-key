# Cloudflare Workers AI — Privacy / Training Policy Evidence

**Last Verified:** 2026-04-27
**Source:** https://developers.cloudflare.com/workers-ai/ + https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/

## Official Quotes

> "Cloudflare does not train its AI models on customer content."
> — Cloudflare Workers AI documentation / DPA (retrieved 2026-04-27 via WebSearch)

> "Customer content processed by Workers AI is not used to train or improve Cloudflare's AI models."
> — Cloudflare Enterprise Terms / DPA (retrieved 2026-04-27)

## Assessment

- Cloudflare: does **not** train on user data processed through Workers AI.
- Main consumer privacy policy focuses on website/CDN usage and does not specifically address Workers AI.
- The Workers AI-specific documentation and DPA confirm no training on customer content.
- Cloudflare has strong enterprise privacy commitments (SOC 2, GDPR).

## Training Status: `false` (Cloudflare does not train on Workers AI user data)

Note: Main privacy policy at cloudflare.com/privacypolicy covers DNS/CDN/consumer products.
For API usage specifically, see Workers AI docs and the Cloudflare DPA.
